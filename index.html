🍎 Educator Setup Notes — Miles, Year 5

  • Allow Miles to self-select his investigation — autonomy is key
  • Scratch projects can be shared via link — low-pressure sharing
  • Screen recording on Chromebook avoids live performance anxiety
  • Mapping tasks suit his love of systems and order
  • Cross-topic projects leverage multiple strengths at once
  • Coding investigations connect to real-world STEM pathways
  • Pre-load scratch.mit.edu and relevant research tabs
  • Build in structured breaks — especially during long coding sessions
  • Celebrate published Scratch projects as real achievements
  • "Teach someone to use Scratch" positions him as classroom expert
  • Data/graphs in transport tasks connect to maths outcomes naturally
  • Set gentle timelines: "share when it feels ready"
